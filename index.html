05 AI & Innovation

Projects

Hackathon · AI 2026

Ground Signal

AI-Powered Community Conflict Detection & Alert System

View on GitHub

Ground Signal is an AI-augmented civic tech platform built to protect communities at risk of conflict. It enables anonymous, low-bandwidth reporting of tensions and rumours via web and USSD — reaching people in areas where internet access is limited. An intelligent rule-based escalation engine analyses incoming signals in real time and automatically alerts Peace Agents when threat patterns cross defined thresholds, enabling faster on-the-ground response before situations deteriorate.

The platform bridges the gap between communities experiencing conflict signals and the NGOs and peace organisations equipped to act on them — all through a lightweight, privacy-first architecture designed for real-world deployment in underserved regions.

▸
Anonymous Reporting
Web and USSD channels ensure anyone can report safely, even without a smartphone or reliable internet.
▸
AI Escalation Engine
Rule-based AI analyses signal frequency and severity, auto-escalating to Peace Agents when risk thresholds are breached.
▸
Peace Agent Dashboard
Dedicated review interface for Peace Agents to triage, verify, and respond to community alerts in real time.
▸
NGO Partner Dashboards
Partner organisations get visibility into conflict signal trends and geographic hotspots to coordinate field response.
